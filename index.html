Jumping Hat Guy

Instructions
- Press SPACE to start the game
- Use the ARROW RIGHT and ARROW LEFT keys to control the guy
- Press SPACE to activate the boost for the next jump
- Press the ARROW UP key to perform an air-jump
- Blue platforms will get you 3 times as high as black ones
- Red platforms will get you 5 times as high as black ones
- Silver platforms will refill your boost
- Jump on red and blue platforms in a row to get bonus points
- The right side of the screen is connected to the left side of the screen
